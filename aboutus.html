About Kumar Pigeon

Kumar Pigeon, a passionate venture founded 5 years ago, is a proudly homegrown Indian business dedicated to the world of pigeons. What started as a humble dream has grown into a trusted name among breeders, fanciers, and bird lovers across the nation.

We specialize not just as traders of high-quality pigeon goods but also as skilled manufacturers who ensure that every product reflects love, research, and deep understanding of pigeon care.

Our team is made up of seasoned pigeon breeders, experienced veterinarians, and dedicated craftsmen who know the value of reliability and care. Every product—be it feed, supplements, housing accessories, or grooming tools—is tried, tested, and trusted.

At Kumar Pigeon, we believe in building relationships with our customers. We don't just sell products; we deliver peace of mind, feathered happiness, and a commitment to help your pigeons thrive.

Whether you’re a seasoned breeder or just starting out, Kumar Pigeon is here to support your journey with trusted products and genuine advice. Thank you for being part of our story—we look forward to growing together!

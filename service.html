Our Services

Beyond products, we offer specialized services for pigeon enthusiasts to help them excel in breeding, racing, and maintaining their flocks.

Custom Ring Design

Customize pigeon rings with names, serial numbers, and colors to reflect your club or personal identity. Choose from durable materials like aluminum or plastic. Great for race tracking and breeding lineage documentation.

Nationwide Delivery

Get our premium products delivered anywhere in India. We partner with reliable logistics providers to ensure your items arrive safely and on time, with real-time tracking and special packaging for delicate items.

Expert Advice

Access one-on-one consultations with pigeon care specialists. We help with breeding plans, disease prevention, and training techniques to keep your pigeons healthy and competitive.

Bulk Orders

Special packages and discounts for clubs, events, and bulk buyers. Get bulk rings, feed, medicines, and accessories with flexible shipping and invoice options. Ideal for organizers and large breeders.

Health Check-ups

Book routine or emergency health assessments for your flock. We organize on-site camps and remote video consultations with avian vets, ensuring your pigeons stay in top form all year.

Training Programs

Learn from the pros. Our training modules cover everything from feeding and conditioning to race strategies and homing techniques. Ideal for both hobbyists and competitive pigeon racers.

Loft Design Consultation

Build the perfect pigeon loft with our expert guidance. From ventilation and hygiene to layout and materials, we help you create an environment that promotes health and performance.

Ready to Elevate Your Pigeon Experience?

Contact our service team today to discuss how we can support your pigeon passion with our specialized services.
